The process

What happens when you hit Scan

We run a full automated audit inside an isolated browser sandbox — no plugins, no extensions, no cache.

STEP 01

Launch Isolated Sandbox

We spin up a clean Daytona browser environment with Playwright. No cookies, no history — a fresh session every time.

STEP 02

Record All Network Traffic

Before touching the cookie banner, we capture every network request the page makes. This is the "before" baseline.

STEP 03

Click "Reject All"

We auto-detect and click the reject button across 6 major consent platforms — OneTrust, Cookiebot, TrustArc, Didomi, and more.

STEP 04

Watch What Still Fires

After rejection we reload and intercept all traffic again. Any tracker that fires after "Reject All" is a GDPR violation.

STEP 05

Cross-Reference Tracker DB

Each domain is checked against the Disconnect.me database of 6,324 known trackers — categorised by type and company.

STEP 06

Generate Full Legal Report

We check if each tracker is disclosed in the cookie policy, calculate the GDPR fine exposure, and draft a ready-to-send DPA complaint.
